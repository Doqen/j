Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 05/13/2022

Endgame News ("Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Endgame News.


1. Personal Information Collected

We collect information that you provide directly to us. For example, we collect information when you create an account, subscribe, participate in any interactive features of our services, fill out a form, request customer support or otherwise communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, postal address, credit card information and other contact or identifying information you choose to provide.


2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including to:

  • Provide, operate, maintain and improve our platform;
  • Enable you to access and use our platform, including uploading, downloading, collaborating on and sharing content;
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages;
  • Provide and deliver the services and features you request, process and complete transactions, and send you related information;
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests and provide customer service and support.


3. Sharing of Your Information

We may share information we receive about you in the following ways:

  • With third-party vendors and other service providers that perform services on our behalf, as needed to carry out their work for us, which may include managing our data, distributing emails, conducting research and analysis, providing customer service and conducting payments.
  • As required by applicable law or legal process;
  • In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company;


4. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.


5. Your Rights

You can review and change your personal information by logging into the Website and visiting your account profile page. You may also notify us to correct or delete any personal information that you have provided to us.


6. Cookies

This website, operated by Endgame News, uses cookies to improve your user experience and to provide us with information about how our website is being used.

Our third-party vendors, including Google, use these cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other websites. This usage of advertising cookies allows Google and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. If you would like to opt out of personalized advertising, you can do so by visiting Google's Ads Settings. Alternatively, you can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Please note that if we have not opted out of third-party ad serving, the cookies of other third-party vendors or ad networks may also be used to serve ads on our site. You can visit the respective third-party vendor and ad network websites to find more about their use of cookies for personalized advertising and how to opt out, if this capability is provided. Alternatively, you can opt out of some third-party vendors’ uses of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info. Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our website.


7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
